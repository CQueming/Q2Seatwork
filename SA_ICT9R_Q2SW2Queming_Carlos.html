DOG BREEDS

IMAGE Breeds Description

American Buldog

American Bulldogs are a well-balanced dog that demonstrate great strength, endurance, agility, and a friendly attitude.The American Bulldog is a descendant of the English Bulldog.

Siberian Husky

 The Siberian Husky has a thickly coated, compact sled dog of medium size and great endurance and was developed to work in packs, pulling light loads at moderate speeds over vast frozen expanses.

Shitzu

. Shi Tsu (pronounced in the West sheet-su'; the Chinese say 'sher-zer' usually weighing between 9 to 16 pounds, and standing between 8 and 11 inches, are surprisingly solid for dogs their size.

Maltese

. The tiny Maltese, 'Ye Ancient Dogge of Malta,' has been sitting in the lap of luxury since the Bible was a work in progress. Famous for their show-stopping, floor-length coat, Maltese are playful, charming, and adaptable toy companions.

Border Collie

A remarkably bright workaholic, the Border Collie is an amazing dog'¿maybe a bit too amazing for owners without the time, energy, or means to keep it occupied.

Pug

. Pugs live to love and to be loved in return. The Pug's motto is the Latin phrase 'multum in parvo' (a lot in a little)'¿an apt description of this small but muscular breed.

German Shepherd

The German Shepherd Dog is a large, agile, muscular dog of noble character and high intelligence. Loyal, confident, courageous, and steady, the German Shepherd is truly a dog lover's delight.

Afghan Hound

 Despite his regal appearance, the Afghan can exhibit an endearing streak of silliness and a profound loyalty.

Alaskan Malamute

 strong, heavy-duty worker of spitz type, the Alaskan Malamute is an affectionate, loyal, and playful but dignified dog recognizable by his well-furred plumed tail carried over the back, erect ears, and substantial bone.

Pomeranian

The tiny Pomeranian, long a favorite of royals and commoners alike, has been called the ideal companion. The glorious coat, smiling, foxy face, and vivacious personality have helped make the Pom one of the world's most popular toy breeds.
